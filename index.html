About Wichita Area Electricians

Founded in 2010, Wichita Area Electricians began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional electrical services to our community. What started as a small operation has grown into the most trusted electrical service provider in the Wichita area. Our success is built on a foundation of hard work, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Over the past decade, we've completed thousands of projects for residential and commercial clients, earning a reputation for excellence that extends throughout Kansas.

We believe in putting our customers first, every single time. Our core values—professionalism, transparency, and quality—guide every decision we make and every job we undertake. We invest in continuous training for our electricians to ensure they're equipped with the latest knowledge and techniques. Whether you need emergency repairs, routine maintenance, or a complete electrical installation, our team is dedicated to exceeding your expectations. We're not just an electrical service; we're your trusted partner in keeping your home or business safe, comfortable, and running smoothly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our electrical services

Do you offer same-day service? expand_more

Yes! We understand that electrical issues can't always wait. We offer same-day service for most requests in the Wichita area. When you call, we'll assess your situation and provide you with an estimated arrival time. For emergency situations, we have 24/7 availability to handle urgent electrical problems that can't wait until business hours.

Are your electricians licensed and insured? expand_more

Absolutely. Every electrician on our team is fully licensed by the state of Kansas and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We maintain current certifications and stay up-to-date with all electrical codes and safety regulations. This means you can have complete peace of mind knowing your electrical work is being handled by qualified professionals who meet all industry standards.

What types of electrical services do you provide? expand_more

We provide comprehensive electrical services for both residential and commercial properties. This includes new installations, repairs, maintenance, circuit breaker work, wiring, outlet and switch installation, lighting design and installation, electrical panel upgrades, troubleshooting and diagnostics, emergency repairs, and much more. Whether it's a simple outlet replacement or a complete commercial electrical system installation, we have the expertise to handle it.

How much does your service cost? expand_more

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. The cost of our service depends on the specific work required. We provide free estimates for most jobs, and we'll explain exactly what you're paying for before we begin any work. Our rates are competitive and reflect the quality of service and expertise we provide. We accept various payment methods and can discuss payment options if needed.

Do you offer any warranties on your work? expand_more

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. The specific warranty terms depend on the type of work performed, but we guarantee that all installations and repairs will be completed to the highest standards. If any issues arise within the warranty period, we'll address them at no additional cost. We're committed to your satisfaction and want you to feel confident in the electrical work we've done for you.
